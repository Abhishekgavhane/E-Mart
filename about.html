Who Are We?


E-Mart.com, Inc. is an American multinational technology company based in Seattle, Washington, which focuses on e-commerce, cloud computing, digital streaming, and artificial intelligence.

E-Mart is known for its disruption of well-established industries through technological innovation and mass scale.

It is the world's largest online marketplace, AI assistant provider, live-streaming platform and cloud computing platform as measured by revenue and market capitalization. E-Mart is the largest Internet company by revenue in the world.It is the second largest private employer in the United States and one of the world's most valuable companies. As of 2020, E-Mart has the highest global brand valuation.
